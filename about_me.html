HI, MY NAME IS...

NEVAEH WILLIAMS

I’m a graphic designer with a passion for creating visually compelling work that blends creativity, strategy, and technology. I have experience in social media design, brand identity and development, and replicating established branding and marketing styles with precision and consistency. With hands-on experience using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Procreate, and Figma — along with working knowledge of Premiere Pro and After Effects — I enjoy bringing ideas to life across both digital and visual platforms.

I hold a degree in Informatics and have a strong interest in coding and technology, which allows me to approach design with both creativity and logic. Graphic design is where my two passions — art and technology — come together. I’m constantly learning, experimenting, and refining my skills, and my drive to grow as a designer only continues to deepen. For me, design isn’t just work — it’s how I create, problem-solve, and tell stories visually.
